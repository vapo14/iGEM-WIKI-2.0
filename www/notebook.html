Protocols: Sterilization, Culture Media Preparation & Overnight Culture

Lab materials were sterilized at 121°C for 15 minutes.

  • 2.0 mL Eppendorf tubes jar (2)
  • 1.5 mL Eppendorf tubes jar (2)
  • 1.0 mL Eppendorf tubes jar (2)
  • 0.5 mL Eppendorf tubes jar (2)
  • L shaped glass spreader (7)
  • Pipettes (4)
  • Filters (5)

  • 2 plates CAM-
  • 8 plates CAM+

CaCl2 (11.09 g) was added to 500 mL and graduated to 1 L.

LB medium (10 mL) was inoculated with 100 μL of NEB 5-alpha.

06.08.2019

Protocols: Sterilization, Culture Media Preparation and Stock of cells

Lab materials and solutions were sterilized at 121ºC for 15 minutes.

  • 2.0 mL Eppendorf tubes jar (2)
  • 1.5 mL Eppendorf tubes jar (2)
  • 1.0 mL Eppendorf tubes jar (2)
  • 0.2 mL Eppendorf tubes jar (2)
  • L shaped glass spreader (7)
  • Pipettes (2)
  • Solution 1: 15 gr sucrose, 0.6057 gr Tris y 1.8612 gr EDTA
  • Solution 3: 29.442 gr KCH3COO y 11.5 mL CH3COOH

Three plates with LB media were prepared:

  • 3 plates CAM+

Seventeen cryogenic vials of E. coli NEB 5-alpha were obtained from 500 μL of glycerol at 40% plus 500 μL of the cells.

Protocol: Transformation
